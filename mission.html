Our Mission



Helping Hands of Tennessee is the only region’s comprehensive oral healthcare provider outside of Memphis, Tennessee in the West Tennessee region. HHT provides dental services for patients from age 2 to 99 - regardless of their ability to pay for dental treatments. The dental clinic provides full general dentistry services based on patient’s income. Fees are based on a four-tier sliding fee scale structure and include services such as dental extractions, composite fillings, removable dentures, crowns, and more. Helping Hands also provides those dental treatments to both insured and uninsured patients who earn 200 percent of the federal poverty level.
