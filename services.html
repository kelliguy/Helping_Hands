Services


At Helping Hands of Tennessee, we provide a list of quality dental services that'll give you the smile you've long waited for! Here are a list of the services we provide including Basic Cleaning all the way to acquiring Dentures.


A Comprehensive Dental Exam involves a complete and thorough patient examination covering everything from pre-existing conditions, gum health, even screenings, and diagnostic exams to guarantee optimal oral health.
Cost: $20


A Limited Dental Exam focuses on particular problems you may be having. Also used to find possible signs of oral or other diseases.
Cost: $20


A Simple Extraction is performed on a tooth that is above the gumline and can be seen in the mouth. This is a quick process, and the healing time is usually pretty fast.
Cost: $45 (per tooth)


A Surgical Extraction is a slightly more complex procedure that occurs when a tooth has not yet broken through the gum line, or has not yet fully grown into the mouth. Often results in a small incision into your gum in order to access the affected tooth.
Cost: $65-85 (per tooth)
