Services


At Helping Hands of Tennessee, we provide a list of quality dental services that'll give you the smile you've long waited for! Here are a list of the services we provide including Basic Cleaning all the way to acquiring Dentures.


A Comprehensive Dental Exam involves a complete and thorough patient examination covering everything from pre-existing conditions, gum health, even screenings, and diagnostic exams to guarantee optimal oral health.
Cost: $20


A Limited Dental Exam focuses on particular problems you may be having. Also used to find possible signs of oral or other diseases.
Cost: $20

A Simple Extraction is performed on a tooth that is above the gumline and can be seen in the mouth. This is a quick process, and the healing time is usually pretty fast.
Cost: $45 (per tooth)


A Surgical Extraction is a slightly more complex procedure that occurs when a tooth has not yet broken through the gum line, or has not yet fully grown into the mouth. Often results in a small incision into your gum in order to access the affected tooth.
Cost: $65-85 (per tooth)


A Basic Cleaning involves dental scaling removes the plaque and tartar from your teeth that you cannot do at home with normal toothbrushing. Also consists of Flossing,Polishing, and Fluoride Treatment.
Cost: $45


A Dental deep cleaning, also known as scaling and root planing, involves special techniques to get rid of plaque, tartar, and bacteria below the gum line down to your tooth roots. This prevents gum disease from advancing and causing tooth loss.
Cost: $60

A Crown, most commonly refers to a dental cap, a type of dental restoration that completely caps or encircles a tooth or dental implant. A crown may be needed when a large cavity threatens the health of a tooth. A crown is typically bonded to the tooth by dental cement.
Cost: $525(temporary crown included)

A Removable Prosthetic, or Dentures, are a removable replacement for missing teeth and surrounding tissues. Two types of dentures are available -- complete and partial dentures. Complete dentures are used when all the teeth are missing, while partial dentures are used when some natural teeth remain.
Cost: $550
